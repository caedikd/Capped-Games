Site FAQ
How do I post my game for testing?
Simply navigate to the test tab from the navigation bar and open up a new thread. There, you can create a new post with your games information and requested testers.
How do I sign up to be a tester?
First navigate to the test tab where you will see a forum of current games being tested. Choose a game that matches your interests and follow the directions listen in the original testers post. If chosen, further instructions will be given by the developer.
Where can I find more information about accessibility in gaming?
You can learn more at our blog tab under "Learn" but we also encourage you to do your own research using credible sources.
Is there anywhere I can view accessibility ratings and scores on current games?
Yes, navigate to our rating tab where you will be able to search through a comprehensive database of current games based on genre and accessibility scores.
